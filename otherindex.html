About Loren Roosendaal

Fascinated by technology from a young age, Loren worked on videogames played by millions before he left high school. Since then Loren has established himself as a serial entrepreneur and leading expert in the field of digital transformation as a lecturer at Nyenrode University and keynote speaker. He brings together the digital and social realm and helps organizations to discover fresh perspectives on topics like learning & development, behaviour & culture and digital businessmodels.

Speaking

Loren’s fascinating life story and deep understanding of the technological world around us, helps him open the eyes of his audience to our new digital reality. Inspiring them to look further and leaving them with valuable insights.

Events

Despite his youthful enthusiasm, Loren has solved complicated digital transformation challenges all around the world. He loves going off script and providing people at your event with stories and live insights about the specific challenges they are facing.

Strategy

Able to analyze problems at lightning speed and draw from a deep understanding of technology and people, Loren loves to help boards and C-level executives make sense of the digital world, allowing them to turn would be risks into opportunities.

Topics

Loren has a deep understanding of the technological world and speaks on a wide range of topics. No matter what the subject of his talk, Loren loves to make the impact of technology on our work and daily life easy to understand with stories and practical examples.

This year Loren has been particularly focused on helping organizations understand the upcoming learning revolution, delivering a series of keynotes, panel sessions and even masterclasses following his “Why learning is broken and how to fix it” TED talk.
